Digital Photo Manipulations

When it was a little warmer outside (fall?) I shot Bug's eye view. Lying down on the ground getting that picture has an interesting story attached to it. Foli(arson)age and Alien Garden are derived from that photograph. Nothing big, just playing around with colour maps. The original photo was modified with The GIMP.


A long long time ago, when I first started using my camera, I was lucky enough to take this photo as a part of the instant stalactites set. Thinking of how a real photo might have degenerated over a very long period of time, I created the Old Plant Photo. The original photo was modified with The GIMP.


On my way to the Ann Arbor art fair in the evening, the sun was at a nice position in the sky giving me a long shadow. Since that picture was basically a black and white photo, I played around with it until Bumble Bee Me was born. The original photo was modified with The GIMP.


Took a few shots at the beach when I was in Miami, Florida. Played around with the colours to get the following. An alien beach and a very cool sun partially obscured by clouds. The original photos were modified with The GIMP.



Well, someone needs a hair cut, and soon. What else can I say? Played around with some filters and color mapped it all alien style to get what I got. The original photos were modified with The GIMP.

I love the colours, but decided to see how it would be if it were red in the center instead of yellow. The original image was modified with The GIMP.
