Boston Trip 2003 [3]

I recently went to M.I.T to give a talk at a computational mechanics conference. It was a fun experience and I liked Boston. I took quite a few pictures on the days I was free and I've uploaded some of them. I've divided them into sets to ease the pain for those on dial-up. The first set contains pictures taken in the M.I.T museum and those taken inside the conference halls (mostly class rooms), so that I dont forget how they looked. The next two sets contains shots of the city, taken mainly along a walk path, called the freedom tour. I had taken outside campus shots of M.I.T and Harvard but messed up and lost them.
