Ann Arbor Art Fair 2003

Went to the Art Fair going on at central campus. I have never seen so many people in Ann Arbor at one time. It was hot, crowded, and a LOT of fun. Tons of things to look at, places to eat, performers on the street, and all sorts of art on display. I had gone to it twice, but only once with my camera. At that time too, I decided to take a video of this comic/performer (just outside the building where my German class used to be) which left very little memory in the camera for too many real pictures .
